Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ticket?

You can book a ticket by selecting a movie and showtime from the homepage. Once you've selected your seat, just complete the payment process.

Can I cancel my booking?

Unfortunately, cancellations are not possible once the ticket is booked. Please make sure the details are correct before confirming the booking.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are processed under certain conditions. Please contact our support team for more details.
