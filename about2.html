ABOUT THE MOVIE:-

Dragon is a 2025 Indian Tamil-language coming-of-age comedy drama film directed by Ashwath Marimuthu, who wrote the story with Pradeep Ranganathan. Produced by AGS Entertainment, it stars Pradeep (as the title character), Anupama Parameswaran and Kayadu Lohar, with Mysskin, Gautham Vasudev Menon, K. S. Ravikumar and George Maryan. In the film, a college student abandons his education and deceptively secures a high paying job, but is forced to clear his arrears upon realising he is in danger of being exposed.
