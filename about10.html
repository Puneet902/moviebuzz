ABOUT THE MOVIE:-

Hari Hara Veera Mallu: Part 1 – Sword vs Spirit is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language historical action adventure film directed by Krish Jagarlamudi and A. M. Jyothi Krishna from a screenplay by Jagarlamudi and Sai Madhav Burra.
